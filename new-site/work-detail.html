Threadless.com redesign

Project Overview

This project involved a reimagining of classic Swiss poster design for a contemporary audience. We applied the principles of grid-based design, clean typography, and balance to ensure the message is communicated effectively.

Process

The project began with research into Swiss design pioneers like Josef Müller-Brockmann. From there, we developed multiple iterations, refining the grid system and experimenting with typography until the final design was achieved.
